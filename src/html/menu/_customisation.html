Ordering Process

  • Consultation: Our team conducts a detailed consultation with each client to find out their taste preferences, style and preferences in organizing the event. We discuss various aspects of the order, including budget, number of guests and menu preferences.

  • Menu selection: Based on the information received during consultations, our experts develop an individual menu that reflects the client’s taste preferences, seasonal products and the theme of the event. We offer a variety of meal options to cater to all guests.

  • Coordination of details: BAfter developing the menu, our team coordinates all orders with the client, including the date and time of the event, location, decor and additional services. We strive for maximum transparency and are ready to answer all the client’s questions to ensure the success of the event.
