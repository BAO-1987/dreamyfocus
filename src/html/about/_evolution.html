Evolution of company

  • 2012

    Expansion of the menu range.

    In 2012, BanquetBites expanded its menu to include new dishes and culinary specialties. This allowed the company to attract more customers with different taste preferences and meet the needs of a wider audience.

  • 2015

    Launch of catering services for corporate events.

    In 2015, BanquetBites launched a specialized catering service for corporate events. This was an important step in the development of the company, allowing it to attract large customers and strengthen its position in the business sector service market.

  • 2017

    Introduction of innovative technologies into the ordering and delivery process.

    In 2017, BanquetBites introduced innovative technologies into the ordering and delivery process, such as online booking and real-time order tracking. This has significantly improved the customer experience and increased service efficiency.

  • 2020

    Expansion into new markets and regions.

    In 2020, BanquetBites expanded into new markets and regions, expanding its presence and increasing the number of partner restaurants and service points. This allowed the company to strengthen its position in the market and attract new customers.
