Awards

  • Opening of the BanquetBites flagship restaurant: 2010.

    In 2010, BanquetBites opened its first flagship restaurant in the heart of the city. This was an important step in the company's development, allowing it to offer its customers a unique culinary experience and popularize the brand in the region.

  • Award "Best Catering Service of the Year": 2015.

    In 2015, BanquetBites was awarded the prestigious Catering Service of the Year award. This is a confirmation of the high quality of the company's services and recognition of its contribution to the field of culinary and service.

  • Launch of the BanquetBites mobile application: 2018.

    In 2018, BanquetBites launched its own mobile app, providing customers with a convenient way to order food and catering services. This has allowed the company to expand its presence in the market and meet the needs of modern customers for mobility and convenience.
